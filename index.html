About Us

Chef Auguste Gusteau

Founder of Gusteau's, a culinary legend, revolutionized French cuisine with his exceptional talent and heartwarming philosophy, "Anyone Can Cook." Beginning his journey in a modest kitchen in Paris, Gusteau's passion for cooking blossomed into a celebrated culinary empire. His groundbreaking cookbook brought gourmet cooking into homes worldwide, while his restaurant became synonymous with culinary excellence, earning multiple Michelin stars and captivating food enthusiasts with its exquisite dishes and impeccable service.

Gusteau's wisdom and vision were encapsulated in his many inspirational quotes. He famously said, "You must be imaginative, strong-hearted. You must try things that may not work, and you must not let anyone define your limits because of where you come from. Your only limit is your soul. What I say is true—anyone can cook... but only the fearless can be great."

Although Chef Gusteau is no longer with us, his legacy endures. His dedication to quality, creativity, and hospitality continues to inspire and shape the culinary world, making Gusteau a timeless icon in the realm of gastronomy.

Chef Alfredo Linguini

The son of the legendary Chef Auguste Gusteau and the current owner of Gusteau's restaurant. Linguini's journey from a modest garbage boy to the helm of one of Paris's most prestigious kitchens is a testament to his resilience, passion, and innate culinary talent. Alfredo Linguini's success is enhanced by the guidance of a remarkable and secretive culinary genius, often referred to as the "Little Chef," who brings extraordinary talent to Gusteau's kitchen.

Under Linguini's leadership, Gusteau's blends traditional French culinary techniques with innovative approaches, keeping the menu both classic and contemporary. His commitment to quality and creativity ensures that every dish reflects the rich heritage of the restaurant.

As Gusteau's continues to enchant diners from around the world, Alfredo Linguini remains dedicated to upholding the values and standards set by his father, while also making his own mark on the culinary world. His journey is a true embodiment of perseverance, passion, and the belief that greatness can come from the most unexpected places.
